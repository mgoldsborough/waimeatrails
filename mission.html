Our Mission
and Values

Our Mission Statement

The mission of Waimea Trails and Greenways is to be an advocate for the creation, maintenance, and sustainability of an accessible pathway that enhances the quality of life by connecting Waimea's people and places.

Our Values

We believe in:

  • Healthy and safe alternatives to cars
  • Encouragement of outdoor exercise opportunities Reduction of traffic congestion and air pollution
  • Fostering aloha, neighborliness, courtesy and civility Urban plans that are pedestrian friendly
  • Community involvement in building and maintaining the pathway
  • Fostering community appreciation, understanding and stewardship over Waimea's streams
